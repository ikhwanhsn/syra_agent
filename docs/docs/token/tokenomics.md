# ✨ Tokenomics

## Token Distribution

Our distribution model prioritizes community ownership and decentralization from day one.

| Allocation    | Percentage | Amount      | Details                                               |
| ------------- | ---------- | ----------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| **Community** | 97%        | 970,000,000 | Public sale                                           |
| **Team**      | 3%         | 30,000,000  | Development, operations, and long-term sustainability |

### Community Allocation (97%)

The vast majority of tokens are allocated to the community to ensure broad distribution and active participation:

- **Public Launch**: Fair launch on Pump.fun with no private sale or pre-sale
- **Liquidity Provision**: Deep liquidity for healthy trading conditions

### Team Allocation (3% - 5%)

A minimal team allocation demonstrates our commitment to community-first values:

- **Total Team Supply**: 3% (30,000,000 tokens)
- **Lock Schedule**: 16M locked for 12 months ([proof](https://app.streamflow.finance/contract/solana/mainnet/8h8dvpkS8Ypa2gKVzGwXfYSKzbTHGuEaNwSj6SbTPzZo))
- **Burn Commitment**: 5M burned immediately post-launch ([proof](https://solscan.io/tx/5eDpknA4FsVcxmxYBb2LFwZgLMitKw3GvYiw6dyHiWPfFtoqdoGuALjpXhxmWFETz28J5oga7f43Z33FNoNByhXX))
- **Available**: for operational expenses and development

:::info Transparency Commitment
All team wallets are publicly disclosed and tracked. Vesting schedules are enforced through smart contracts with no early unlock provisions.
:::

$SYRA serves multiple purposes within the Syra ecosystem:

### 1. **Premium Features Access**

- Access to Custom Agent Builder
- Priority support and early feature access
- Premium API rate limits for developers

### 2. **Governance Rights**

- Vote on protocol upgrades and new features
- Propose new trading strategies and integrations
- Participate in treasury allocation decisions
- Influence roadmap priorities

### 3. **Staking & Rewards**

- Stake $SYRA to earn passive rewards
- Boosted rewards for long-term stakers
- Revenue sharing from platform fees
- Exclusive airdrops and ecosystem benefits

### 4. **Agent Marketplace**

- Pay for premium custom trading agents
- Earn fees by creating and sharing strategies
- Tip top-performing agents in Battle Arena
- Access exclusive agent NFTs and achievements

### 5. **Fee Discounts**

- Reduced trading fees for $SYRA holders
- Discounted subscription plans
- Lower costs for API usage
- Priority execution on autonomous trades

### 6. **Ecosystem Participation**

- Participate in Battle Agent Arena competitions
- Unlock achievement badges and XP multipliers
- Access to exclusive community events
- Early access to new token listings and features

## Sustainability Mechanisms

### Deflationary Measures

- **Transaction Burns**: Small percentage of trading fees x402 burned permanently
- **Buyback Program**: Protocol revenue used for periodic token buybacks
- **Unused Rewards**: Unclaimed rewards redistributed or burned quarterly

### Revenue Model

Platform sustainability is ensured through multiple revenue streams:

- Trading signal subscriptions
- Premium API access fees
- Agent Marketplace transaction fees (small percentage)
- Enterprise licensing
- Partnership integrations

A portion of all revenue flows back to token holders through staking rewards and buyback programs.
