What this tool does

Why pay-per-call

  • One wallet — USDC payer covers news, on-chain intel, and partner routes.
  • Agent-native — HTTP 402 auto-pay mid-task; no human key pasting.
  • Transparent price — exact charge in the 402 accepts[] array.
